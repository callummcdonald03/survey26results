How to read eNPS. Employee Net Promoter Score, or eNPS, is a simple way to show the balance of sentiment. It does not average every score. Instead, it looks at the share of colleagues who are strongly positive and subtracts the share who are less positive.

That means Passives are still shown in the charts, but they do not add to or subtract from the final eNPS number. When we say a score is higher or lower than 2025, we mean eNPS points: the movement between the 2025 eNPS number and the 2026 eNPS number.
